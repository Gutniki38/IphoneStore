What's new

All models are equipped with the A16 Bionic chip, a 48-megapixel main camera, and ProMotion displays with Ceramic Shield protection and a refresh rate range of 1 to 120 Hz, as well as accelerated LPDDR5 memory. The design of the lineup has been updated, with two notches on the front panel of the smartphones.

All iPhone 14 models in the U.S. will be sold without a SIM card tray: the manufacturer claims that the enhanced eSIM technology will allow users to transfer their old eISMs to new smartphones.

The front camera has been updated (it now features autofocus and recognizes the user faster in low-light conditions), as well as the main camera sensor.

The iPhone 14 Pro features a new design — without the signature "notch." It also comes in a new color option. Unlike the regular iPhone 14 models, the iPhone 14 Pro is equipped with the A16 Bionic chip, which can perform 4 trillion photo operations per second.
